About the Walaaybaa Rangers

The Walaaybaa Ranger Program is the on-Country ranger team of the Tamworth Local Aboriginal Land Council (TLALC). The Rangers care for Country across Kamilaroi / Gamilaroi / Gomeroi lands — combining cultural knowledge held by Elders and Traditional Owners with contemporary land and water management practice.

Their work spans cultural burning, native seed and habitat regeneration, water and river health, feral animal and weed management, cultural heritage protection, and bringing young people through as the next generation of land and sea managers.

The Walaaybaa Rangers are proud hosts of the Ngarringilanha Ranger Gathering — a place for ranger groups, IPA teams, Elders, Traditional Owners and partners to share knowledge, strengthen networks and put Country and culture first.

Getting to Trelawney

Trelawney Station
Menedebri Road, Somerton NSW 2340

Trelawney is an Aboriginal-owned working property about 40 minutes from Tamworth. Roads on the property are unsealed in places — drive to the conditions.

On arrival

①
Stop at the gate / induction deskEveryone entering site must complete and sign an induction before going further. No exceptions.
②
Drive slowly — 40 km/h on property10 km/h past the entry point. Working property with cattle and a private residence — leave gates as you find them.
③
Park only in the formal parking areaMove off and onto the formed road only. A bus moves groups between stations during the day.

Site safety — the essentials

⚑
Induction is mandatoryEveryone on site must be inducted and signed in before taking part.
▲
Drive slow — 40 km/h, 10 km/h past the entryVehicles stay on formed roads. No pedestrian access outside the main activity area.
≋
Water is NOT drinkableTrelawney runs on rain and river water. Drink only the supplied potable water. Keep showers short.
✷
FireNo fires or smoking in camping grounds — designated areas only. Up to five managed fire pits; everyone helps keep an eye on them. Butts in bins.
⌗
Watch your stepUneven ground, tent pegs, and snakes/spiders around the woodpile at the chemical-storage fence. Carry only manageable loads of wood.
◷
AlcoholDesignated areas only, at set times (meals & evening). None after midnight. Non-alcoholic options always available. A sober Duty Officer is on call.
♺
Waste & gatesAll rubbish in bins; only toilet paper flushed; sanitary bins provided. Leave every gate as you found it.
